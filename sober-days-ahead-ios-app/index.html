This is an iOS App / Widget I wrote a few years ago, and really need to update soon.

The Gist...

I stopped drinking on the first of April, 2015.    Yeah, I was (am?) an alcoholic, but that's not the point of this.    I think it was when I was coming up on 2000 days sober that I thought it would be neat to have an app & widget on my phone that did little more than show me in real terms how long I'd been dry.

You'll sometimes hear me say "damn I could go for a drink."   It's true, sometimes after a series of particular shitty days, I'll have that back-of-the-mind desire to just get smashed and not think about things.  Now, I'm not about to break my sobriety over something stupid like work stress, or relationship stress, or being broke stress, or really anything.   That said, it's a lot easier when I can look at my phone and quickly see that I'd be throwing away 3080 days (as of this edit) just to blow off some steam.

The widget screenshot there on the right was taken 06Sep2023, while the three example screenshots below were from my initial App Store upload back in 2020.  Wild coincidence, I took those screenshots on 10Sep2020, so nearly three years ago to the day!

The app is available 100% free on the iOS App Store, click the App Store logo below for a link to download.    I don't know the first thing about writing Android Apps, so it's iOS exclusive at this point.   

And if you're thinking "hey, a potential employer could read this."  Well, I'm not ashamed for anyone to know that I had a drinking problem in my 30s. Besides, I wouldn't want to work for anybody that would judge me over mistakes I've clearly overcome, so we'll leave it at that.
